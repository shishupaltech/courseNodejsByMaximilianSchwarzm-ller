cookies 
Great for storing data on the client (browser);
Do not store sensitive data here! it can be viewed + manipulated 

cookies can be configured to expire when the browser is closed(=> "Session Cookie") or when a certain age / expiry date is reached ("permanent cookies)
.works well together with session.

Session 
. Stored on the server, Not on the client 

. Great for storing sensitive data that should surive across requests 
you can store anything in sessions often used for storing user data / authentication status 
. Indetified via cookie(don't mistake this with the term "Session Cookie")

you can use different storage for saving your sessions on the server.
